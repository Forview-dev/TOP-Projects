Hi, I’m Alex — born and raised in France, and currently on a renewed journey into the world of coding. My first brush with computer science was about 20 years ago, on the family computer. All of that translated into me getting into a CS curriculum, but at the time, life pulled me in other directions, and my passion for programming went into hibernation.
Fast forward to today, and I’ve found myself re-ignited with that same spark, thanks to discovering The Odin Project (TOP). It’s been a real turning point — a reminder of how much I enjoy building, experimenting, and solving problems through code. I’m starting this new adventure with the goal of developing all the ideas I’ve been carrying around for years, and I can’t wait to see them come to life.
Whether it’s tinkering with a personal project, learning something new, or collaborating with like-minded people, I’m here to grow as a developer and share the journey along the way.
